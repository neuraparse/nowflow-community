# Code of Conduct

NowFlow Community should be a practical, respectful place to build automation
software together.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps the project:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be direct about technical disagreements without personal attacks.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions when something is unclear.
- Respect maintainers' time by keeping issues and pull requests focused.
- Avoid sharing private user data, secrets, customer details, or internal URLs.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, insults, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing someone else's private information.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Spam, deceptive reports, or knowingly unsafe instructions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or limit
participation when behavior harms the project or community.

To report a conduct concern, email conduct@nowflow.io. Security vulnerabilities
should be reported through the process in `SECURITY.md`.
